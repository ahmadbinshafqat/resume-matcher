                Frequently Asked Questions

                Everything you need to know about JobFit AI

                How does the AI job matcher work?
                JobFit AI sends your saved profile (Upwork or Resume) alongside the job description to a large language model. The AI evaluates technical skill overlap, years of experience, leadership signals, and domain fit — then returns a 0–100 match score with detailed skill gap analysis and actionable recommendations.
                What profiles are supported?
                Two profile types are supported: your Upwork freelancer profile (bio, skills, portfolio) and your traditional resume. Both are stored securely as server-side environment variables and never exposed to the browser.
                How do I use the Interview Prep feature?
                After running a match analysis, click the "Interview Prep" tab. The AI generates a tailored elevator pitch, 8–10 likely interview questions, key talking points from your profile, technical topics to review, smart questions to ask the interviewer, and proactive responses to potential concerns.
                Can I analyze jobs not listed on the board?
                Yes. In the Analyze Match section, simply paste any job description manually into the text area and click "Analyze Match". The job board cards are a convenience shortcut — the analyzer works with any JD from any source.
                Is JobFit AI free to use?
                Yes. JobFit AI is completely free. It is powered by Groq's ultra-fast LLaMA inference. You only need a free Groq API key (available at console.groq.com) to self-host it.
